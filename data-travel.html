What Is A Data Packet?

When you open a website, your request is divided into many tiny pieces called Data Packets. Each packet carries a small amount of information and travels through different networks before reaching the destination server.

After processing, the server sends thousands of packets back to your browser, which combines them to display the complete webpage.
